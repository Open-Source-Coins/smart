Features

Fast Transactions

Even faster than Bitcoin, SmartCoin's blazing speed 30 second confirmations will get your payments sent or received anywhere in the world without waiting around.

Encryption Security

With built-in SHA-256 wallet encryption, you can safely store your SmartCoins on your hardware, or even print out your own paper wallet.

Open Record

Transactions can be viewed in a public database, so there is no uncertainty about the status of payment transfers, and this database exists on all clients that use the software.

Quick & Global

No need to wait for transactions. SmartCoin works globally and constantly, which means coins can be sent from everywhere to everywhere at any point in time.

Growing Community

SmartCoin has a growing and friendly community in which everyone can participate and contribute to improve and distribute SmartCoin.

Amazing Efficiency

Transaction fees for sending even large amounts of money are virtually unnoticeable. Save money on financial overhead fees in traditional banking.

How It Works

Acquire Smartcoins.


There are several ways to get SmartCoins. They can either be bought and traded on exchanges like Cryptsy, or earned by participating in our growing community. For new users there's also a faucet, which gives out a small amount of SmartCoins to get you introduced. It is also possible to mine SmartCoins with your computer.

Download wallet and use SmartCoin


Obtain your wallet for your operating system from one of the provided links. Once you're finished with the installation and synchronization process, which takes a few minutes, you're ready to recieve and send SmartCoins. Feel free to join our community to ask questions, contribute or inspire everyone with your ideas!

What is SmartCoin?

With lightning fast transaction times, real-world uses, transparent development, and world-class security, SmartCoin is the practical digital currency. In the age of smartphones, smartcars, smarthouses, and more, we think your currency should be smart too. That’s why we’ve designed SmartCoin to integrate with everyday life. From jobs to purchases and everything inbetween, SmartCoin continues to develop real-world applications with a single goal in mind: creating an accessible, transparent digital currency for all to use.

What are the specs of SmartCoin?

PoW Algorithm: X11
Block Interval: 2 minutes
Difficulty Retarget: Dark Gravity Wave
Subsidy Distribution: Coin subsidy halves every year after block 300,000
Total Coin Supply: ~24.5 million coins

Who is behind SmartCoin?

SmartCoin is an opensource community project. Everyone can participate and contribute to SmartCoin. Anonymous developers like "Psionin" have made enormous contributions to this coin.
